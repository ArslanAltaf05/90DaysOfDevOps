

DevOps:
DevOps is a culture where all roles are collaborated that are involved in the development and maintenance.



Automation:
Automation is a technology where human input is minimized.
Scaling:
Scaling is measure in which a piece of software is grow or shrink.
Infrastructure:
Infrastructure refers to combined components needed for the operation and management.


Why DevOps is Important??

1) It shorten the development cycle and can done faster innovation
2) Reduce failure, reflections and recovery time.
